Privacy Policy for Demos in my Steam Wishlist

Information We Collect

The only information our extension reads is the App Ids of games displayed on Steam wishlist pages (https://store.steampowered.com/wishlist/*).

No personal information is accessed.

How We Use Information

The information collected (App Ids) is used solely for the purpose of cross-referencing with our extension's own database, in order to produce metadata and enhance the functionality of our extension.

We do not store, process, or share this information with any third parties.

Policy Changes

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes made will be reflected on this page.
